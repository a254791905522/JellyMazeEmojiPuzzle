About The Game

Jelly Maze: Emoji Puzzle is a cozy maze adventure built around simple one-swipe movement and thoughtful route planning. Guide a soft jelly hero through pastel puzzle stages, collect blooms, avoid hazards, and discover the best path to the exit. Each level is easy to understand at a glance, but finishing efficiently takes timing, observation, and a little creativity. With offline play, no ads, and a calm visual style, it is designed to feel relaxing while still giving players a satisfying puzzle challenge.

Key Features

  • 50 relaxing maze stages with smooth difficulty growth
  • One-swipe movement that feels simple, clean, and satisfying
  • Bloom collection, stars, and local progress saving
  • Offline play with no ads and no in-app purchases

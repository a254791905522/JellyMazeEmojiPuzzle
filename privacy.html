Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

Jelly Maze: Emoji Puzzle is designed as a fully offline single-player game. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through the app. The game does not require account creation, login, or online connectivity.

Local Data

The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, star ratings, settings, and other save data required for normal gameplay. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

Jelly Maze: Emoji Puzzle does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, behavioral tracking, or in-app purchases.

Permissions

The app is built to run without requesting unnecessary personal-data permissions or network access for gameplay.

Children’s Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal information and works fully offline, it is suitable for a broad audience, including children, subject to parental guidance and local store age ratings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user personal data because the app does not collect that data.

Changes To This Policy

If the privacy practices for the app change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about Jelly Maze: Emoji Puzzle, please use the contact method associated with the app’s store listing or official support page.
